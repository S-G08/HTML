Welcome to Paradise Island!

Destination Overview

Paradise Isliand is a tropical paradise located in the heart of Pacific Ocean. With its crystal clear water and lush landscapes, it's dream destination for travellers seeking adventure and relaxation. Whether you want to unwind on sun-kissed beaches or embark on thrilling outdoor activities, Paradise Island has something for everyone.

Paradise Island is a secluded oasis far removed from hustle and bustle of everyday life. The air is filled with sweet scent of tropical flowers, and the gentle rustle of palm leaves provides a soothing soundtrack to your escape. As you step onto the shores, you are greeted by the warm embrace of the tropical sun and the rythmic lull of the ocean waves.

The coral reefs that surround Paradise Island are the kaleidoscope of colors, housing an abundance of marine life that beckons snorkelers and divers to explore their vibrant underwater world. From playful dolphins to graceful sea turtles, the marine inhabitants add to the allure of this aquatic paradise

Activities

  • Snorkelling in the vibrant coral reefs
  • Relaxing on pristine white sand beaches
  • Exploring the island's tropical rainforests
  • Participating in water sports such as kayaking and paddleboarding

Nearby Attractions

  3. Explore the local markets and sample tropical fruits

Travel Tips

Best time to visit
Year-round but peak season is during the dry months from June to September
Consider visiting during weekdays for fewer crowds
Currency
Local Currency is Paradise Dollars(PD)
Major Credit Cards are widely accepted
Language
English and Paradisean Creole are widely spoken
Learn a few local phrases to enhance your interaction with the locals
Local Cuisine
Indulge in the rich flavors of Paradise Island's local cuisine, feauturing a fusion of seafood, tropical fruits and aromatic spices.
Try the traditional dish called "Island Delight" for a true taste of local flavors
Weather Conditions
While the weather is generally sunny, ocassional tropical showers can occur. It's advisable to carry a light raincoat or umbrella
Check the weather forecast before planning outdoor activities
